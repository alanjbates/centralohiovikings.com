New Club Location and a Vikings Ticket Raffle!

You asked for more comfortable seating, YOU GOT IT!
You asked for better beer prices, YOU GOT IT!

We are excited to announce that the Central Ohio Minnesota Vikings Fan Club has moved to the Blarney Stone at 2151 W Dublin Granville Rd, Worthington, OH 43085.  We have moved away from Gallo's Tap Room due to unforeseen circumstances but wish Gallo's all the best!

The Blarney Stone has a great menu with freshly made food and great sunday drink specials that everyone will enjoy.  Buckets of 6 domestic beers are $12 and the wings are amazing!  They also offer a great craft beer list and authentic Irish food!  Check out CentralOhioVikings.com for more details!

2015 Kick Off Prizes!

We will be raffling off a pair of Lower Bowl Tickets to see the Vikings take on the Lions at Ford Field in Detroit on December 14th!

If you don't want to be a part of the raffle DONT WORRY!  Just wear your purple gear and you will be automatically entered to win other prizes like a Piece of the Metrodome Roof or food voucher!
