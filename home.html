  • Where do we meet?

    Since the 2018 season we have been at our best location ever, Fenders in the Polaris area in Wetserville, Ohio. Come on out for every Vikings game and enjoy weekly food and drink specials along with 30-110 other Vikings fans!!

    You will find us each week at Fenders Bar in the Polaris area
    8333 Market Exchange Dr, Westerville, OH 43081
